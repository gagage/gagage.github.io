The demand for almonds is increasing all over the world every year. This lead Foreign investors to look for new locations to set up almond orchards. Georgia has an extraordinary opportunity to become a hot point for the almond production: the climate and the soil of the eastern Georgia makes excellent conditions to commercially grow almonds.
